Homemade Lasanga

Description

A homemade lasgna recipe with a meaty, made-from-scratch tomato sauce and deliciously cheesy filling. Whereas a more traditional homemade lasgna filling would be made with ricotta cheese this recipe calls for a blend of small-curd cottage cheese and parmesan. Served with a leafy green salad and crusty garlic bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground meat (1/2 pund ground pork, 1/2 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 1 (8 oz.) can of tomato sacue
  • 1 (28 oz) can of crused tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
  • 1 clove of crusehd garlic
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  • 1/8 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 (16 oz.) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound small-curd cottage cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 (16 oz.) package shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Combine pork and ground beef in alarge, deep skillet over medium-high heat; cook and stir until browned and crumbly, 5-7 minutes. Add onion and cook until translucent, about 5 minutes.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, garlic, basil, salt, oregano, and sugar. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer, stirring occasionally, for 30 minutes.
  3. While the sauce is simmering, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in the boiling water, stirring ccasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite. 8-10 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  4. While the noodles are cooking, preheat the oven to 375 degrees F
  5. Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, remaining 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, salt, and pepper in a large bowl until combined.
  6. Assemble lasagna: Spread a spoon or two of sauce over the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish just to cat it. Place two layers of noodles over the sauce to cover. Layer with 1/2 of the cheese mixture, 1/2 of the remaining sauce, and 1/2 of the mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers once more using remaining noodles, cheese mixture, sauce, and mozzarella. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-40 minutes. Remove the foil and bake until cheese is golden brown, 5-10 more minutes.
  8. Remove from the oven and let stand for 10 minutes before cutting and serving.
